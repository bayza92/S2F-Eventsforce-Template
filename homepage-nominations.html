Nominations for the awards

The search is on for the heroes of English golf; the people, clubs and counties who make the game great.

We have 7 categories that you are able to enter whether a club, coach, volunteer or player. To view the categories in more detail click here. Entries can be made by anyone so if you think a club or someone else deserves to be recognised for excellent work in 2017 then here is the place to enter.
